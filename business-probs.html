Business Problems

We focused on two major problems that we felt were Comcast's biggest problems: their consumption model, and their customer service.

  • Consumption Model:

    Currently, cable users, on average, subscribe to over 180 channels when in reality, the consumer only wants 17 on average.

  • Customer Service:

    Comcast currently has poor customer service because call agents are incentivized and trained to not help the customer, and process and procedures prevent the agent from serving customer needs.
